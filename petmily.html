Petmily

UI/UX Design

Petmily is a compound word of pet+family. It’s an application for all abandoned animals to help them find a new family. All abandoned animal shelters can register their animals and upload basic information, such as name, neuter info, breed of animal, age, etc. Users who qualify for adoption conditions can adopt an animal, and also users can donate or volunteer.
